Kiosk manifest setup and more information:

Open Kiosk
Go to the bottom of the page
Click Import policy and load the kiosk-policy.json file that was downloaded
Hit Save & Restart

Note:

The webviews are loaded into kiosk:
~/services/redir/basepage | The first thing that appears when you start kiosk, with a URL bar, and menu buttons. The URL bar does NOT load requested pages in a proxy (no-proxy is ideal for schoolwork, non-blocked pages, and when you are on a HOME/Mobile Hotspot network), HU is meant for loading websites within a proxy.
Google Groups | LCTapps Greyhat Group (must be logged into nv.ccsd.net gAccount)
Element Messenger | Discord Fallback
HU | Loads websites such as Discord in a PROXY to evade CCSD Wi-Fi firewalls.
LCTsearch | Search Engine by LCTapps. Links do not load content in a web proxy, HU does that.
Also, Kiosk is a Legacy Chrome Packaged Application, forwhich GoGuardian cannot see your activity in that application due to GoGuardian being confined into a chrome extension
Note whatever pages you surf on in Google Chrome will be seen by GoGuardian still
You're safe in Kiosk
